The number of people with dementia is growing rapidly, but there is little national provision for them and they rely on informal caregivers such as friends and family. # â“.  What is Escort



Escort is an app that allows people who have family members with dementia or who want to help seniors with dementia to sign up as companions on the app, creating a global network of people and its own hotline.
